# Session 15: Transforming Trading Skills into a Business

---

## Tax Efficiency: Hobby vs Business

### As a Hobbyist
- Earn income at day job
- Pay taxes first
- Buy equipment with after-tax money
- No deductions for trading expenses

### As a Business
- Earn income from trading
- Deduct expenses first
- Pay taxes on remaining profit
- Computer, software, education = deductible

> *"It's how much you get to keep that determines your wealth."*

## Business Structure Basics

### Simplest Options
| Structure | Features |
|-----------|----------|

| LLC | Limited liability, flexibility |

### When to Transition

## Raise Standards, Not Goals

> *"Raising dreams or goals higher is absolutely useless. Instead, refuse to lose more than a certain amount."*

### Wrong Approach
- Goal: Make $10,000/month
- Reality: Never hit it, feel bad
- Response: Raise to $20,000
- Result: More anxiety

### Right Approach
- Standard: Never lose more than 36 pips
- Reality: Achievable daily
- Response: Focus on process
- Result: Consistent improvement

## Operating Schedule

### Daily Routine
- Start time: Fixed (10:00 AM ET)
- Stop time: Fixed (12:00 PM ET)
- Dress: Professional (no underwear trading)
- Morning routine: Same every day
- Evening review: Track results

### Pay Yourself
> *"When's the last time your boss skipped paying you? Never."*

- Pick a payday (e.g., 22nd of month)
- Pay yourself consistently
- Don't skip or delay
- Treat it seriously
